Latitude and Weather

The purpose of this project was to determine the relationship between latitude and weather around the world. Using the OpenWeatherMap API, weather data was collected on over 500 different cities around the globe. Then, the data for temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed were each plotted against latitude.

The goal was to see any relationship between the location of each city relative to the equator, and any of these weather variables. The visualizations were generated using matplotlib and are accessible by clicking any of the images in the visualization tabs, or in the navigation bar under "plots". The data used can also be viewed by clicking the data tab. The charts can be viewed together by clicking the comparisons tab
